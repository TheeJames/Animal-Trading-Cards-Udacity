Peregrine

The Peregrine can dive at speeds of 220mph

  • Area: Coastal Bird
  • Food: Feeds on other Birds
  • Type: North American Falcon
  • Sexual Preference: Monogamist

The Peregrine is a elusive bird that stalks mostly ducks as well as other smaller birds. The Peregrine is also a Monogamist bird like the Red-tail hawk and the Bald Eagle
